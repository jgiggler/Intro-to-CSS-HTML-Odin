Meat Pie

A savory pie that requires 20 cooking to make

Ingredients

  • bowl of water
  • pot of flour
  • pie dish
  • raw meat

Steps

  1. Use the bowl of water on the pot of flour and create pastry dough
  2. Use the pastry dough on the pie dish to create a pie shell
  3. cook the raw meat and use the resulting cooked meat on the pie shell to create an uncooked pie
  4. Cook it in an oven to create the Meat pie
