Pineapple Pizza

A pineapple pizza that requires 65 cooking to make

Ingredients

  • bowl of water
  • pot of flour
  • tomato
  • cheese
  • knife
  • pineapple

Steps

  1. Use the bowl of water on the pot of flour, and create a pizza base.
  2. Use the tomato on the pizza base to create an incomplete pizza
  3. Use the cheese on the incomplete pizza to create an uncooked pizza
  4. Cook it in the oven to create a plain pizza
  5. Use the knife to slice the pineapple to create chunks, and use them on the plain pizza

Congratulations! Now you have made a Pineapple Pizza
