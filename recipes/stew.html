Stew

A meat and potato stew requiring 25 cooking to make

Ingredients

  • bowl
  • raw potato
  • cooked meat

Steps

  1. Fill the bowl with water
  2. Use the resulting bowl of water with raw potato to get an incomplete stew
  3. Use the stew with cooked meat to get an uncooked stew
  4. Cook the stew in the oven

Congratulations! You have made a Stew
